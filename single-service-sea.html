Ocean Freight

Chart a new course for your freight. Reap the benefits of worldwide capacity, reliable sailing times and competitive rates to get your cargo where it needs to be, when it needs to be there. Whether it's less-than-container loads, full container loads or over-sized cargo, our problem solvers at UPS can connect the dots between carriers, ports, customs, your vendors and trucking/rail service providers. And set sail with the peace of mind that you're shipping with one of the leading Non-Vessel Operating Common Carriers (NVOCC). Contact UPS Supply Chain Solutions

Established, Reliable Services

Choose from a full range of ocean freight services that only the world's largest integrated carrier can offer. At Cross World COURIER SERVICES, we strive to provide transparent, predictable and on-time ocean services. Your cargo is monitored every step of the way using visibility information fed continuously by carriers and UPS's global operating systems.

Container Load Management

Maximise routing and transit time for full container loads (FCL) with frequent departures at competitive rates from all ports around the world. Don't have enough cargo for a full container? Less-than-container load (LCL) options are also available between major ports. Space within a container is shared with similarly situated customers' goods to deliver economical usage-based rates while providing Full Container Load (FCL) frequency, routing and visibility.
